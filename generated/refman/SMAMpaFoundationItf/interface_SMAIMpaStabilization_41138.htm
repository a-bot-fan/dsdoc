Enumerated Types


o StabilizationType 
enum StabilizationType {
  NoStabilization,
  Damping,
  EnergyFraction,
  Propagated
}
The type of stabilization.
Parameters:
NoStabilization
No artificial stabilization is included in the step.
Damping
Automatic stabilization based on a damping factor.
EnergyFraction
Automatic stabilization based on a dissipated energy fraction.
Propagated
Continue adaptive stabilization from the immediately preceding general analysis step by carrying over the damping factors from the end of the last step.

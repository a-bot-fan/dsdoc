Enumerated Types


o AcousticCouplingType 
enum AcousticCouplingType {
  On,
  Projection,
  Off
}
The acoustic coupling type.
Parameters:
On
Account for structural-acoustic coupling during the frequency extraction.
Projection
Project structural-acoustic coupling operators onto the subspace of eigenvectors during the frequency extraction.
Off
No structural-acoustic coupling.

SimDof (Enumeration)

enum SimDof {
  SimInvalidDOF,
  SimTranslation1,
  SimTranslation2,
  SimTranslation3,
  SimRotation1,
  SimRotation2,
  SimRotation3
}
The degree of freedom.
Values:
SimInvalidDOF
The invalid degree of freedom.
SimTranslation1
The translation in the first direction of the axis system.
SimTranslation2
The translation in the second direction of the axis system.
SimTranslation3
The translation in the third direction of the axis system.
SimRotation1
The rotation around the first direction of the axis system.
SimRotation2
The rotation around the second direction of the axis system.
SimRotation3
The rotation around the third direction of the axis system.
